# Common Casting Defects and Solutions

## Porosity
**Description**: Internal or surface voids in castings caused by gas entrapment or shrinkage.

### Gas Porosity
- **Causes**: Excessive moisture in sand/mold, inadequate venting, high pouring temperature, dissolved gas in molten metal
- **Visual**: Rounded, smooth-walled holes, often near the surface
- **Solutions**:
  - Reduce moisture content in sand/mold
  - Improve venting in mold design
  - Degas molten metal (argon/nitrogen purging)
  - Reduce pouring temperature
  - Optimize gating system design

### Shrinkage Porosity
- **Causes**: Insufficient feed metal, improper riser placement, incorrect solidification sequence
- **Visual**: Irregular, jagged cavities typically in thicker sections
- **Solutions**:
  - Increase riser size and proper placement
  - Use chills to control solidification direction
  - Modify casting design (uniform wall thickness)
  - Optimize pouring temperature and speed

## Cold Shuts
**Description**: Discontinuity where two molten metal streams fail to fuse properly.
- **Causes**: Low pouring temperature, inadequate gating, insufficient fluidity
- **Solutions**: Increase pouring temperature, improve gating design, increase pouring speed

## Misrun
**Description**: Incomplete filling of the mold cavity.
- **Causes**: Low pouring temperature, insufficient metal, inadequate venting
- **Solutions**: Increase pouring temperature and speed, improve venting

## Sand Inclusions
**Description**: Sand particles embedded in the casting surface.
- **Causes**: Mold erosion, loose sand, improper mold compaction
- **Solutions**: Improve mold quality, reduce pouring velocity, apply mold coating

## Hot Tears
**Description**: Crack formed during solidification due to tensile stress.
- **Causes**: Restrained contraction, hot spots, poor casting design
- **Solutions**: Modify fillet radii, use collapsible cores, adjust pouring temperature

## Shrinkage
**Description**: Depression or cavity from inadequate feeding.
- **Causes**: Incorrect riser design, improper solidification
- **Solutions**: Optimize riser placement, use insulating sleeves, apply chills
