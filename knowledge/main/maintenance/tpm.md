# TPM — Total Productive Maintenance

## Eight Pillars of TPM

### 1. Autonomous Maintenance (Jishu Hozen)
- Operator cleaning, inspection, and lubrication
- Establish basic equipment conditions
- Create and follow standards
- Train operators on equipment knowledge
- Step-by-step progression (7 steps)

### 2. Planned Maintenance
- Preventive maintenance schedules
- Predictive maintenance techniques
- Maintenance standards and checklists
- Spare parts management
- Maintenance KPI tracking (MTBF, MTTR)

### 3. Focused Improvement (Kaizen)
- Reduce Six Big Losses
- Cross-functional improvement teams
- Root cause analysis (5-Why, Fishbone)
- Poka-Yoke implementation
- Standardization of improvements

### 4. Early Equipment Management
- Maintenance prevention design
- Lifecycle cost optimization
- New equipment commissioning
- Installability and maintainability review
- Standardized handover process

### 5. Quality Maintenance
- Zero defect conditions
- Condition-based quality checks
- Defect mapping and analysis
- Process capability monitoring (Cp/Cpk)
- Source inspection systems

### 6. Training and Education
- Skills matrix for operators and technicians
- Multi-skilling program
- Certification system
- Knowledge sharing and documentation
- Cross-training schedule

### 7. Safety, Health, Environment
- Hazard identification and risk assessment
- Lock-out/Tag-out (LOTO) procedures
- Machine guarding standards
- Chemical safety (SDS)
- Ergonomic assessment
- Environmental compliance

### 8. Office TPM
- Administrative process improvement
- Information flow optimization
- Supply chain coordination
- Communication standards
- Visual management in offices

## Key Metrics
- MTBF (Mean Time Between Failures)
- MTTR (Mean Time To Repair)
- Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)
- Planned Maintenance Ratio
- Breakdown Frequency
- Spare Parts Turnover
