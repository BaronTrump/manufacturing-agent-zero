# Manufacturing Safety Standards

## Machine Guarding (OSHA 1910.212)
- Point of operation guarding
- Power transmission guarding
- Fixed, interlocked, adjustable, and self-adjusting guards
- Presence-sensing devices (light curtains, safety mats)

## Lock-Out/Tag-Out (OSHA 1910.147)
- Energy isolation procedures
- Lock application and removal
- Group lockout procedures
- Periodic inspection (annual)
- Training requirements

## Hazard Communication (GHS)
- Safety Data Sheets (SDS)
- Chemical labeling
- Employee training
- Written program

## Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- Safety glasses — mandatory in all production areas
- Steel-toed boots — mandatory in all production areas
- Hearing protection — required above 85 dBA
- Gloves — task-specific (cut-resistant, chemical, thermal)
- Respirators — when engineering controls insufficient

## Confined Space Entry
- Permit-required confined spaces
- Atmospheric testing (O2, LEL, toxic gases)
- Attendant and entry supervisor
- Rescue plan and equipment

## Ergonomics
- NIOSH lifting equation
- Force, repetition, posture assessment
- Engineering controls for high-risk tasks
- Job rotation and rest breaks

## Emergency Procedures
- Evacuation routes and assembly points
- Fire extinguisher locations and types
- First aid and AED locations
- Emergency contact numbers
- Spill response procedures
