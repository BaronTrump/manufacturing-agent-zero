# IATF 16949:2016 — Automotive Quality Management System

## Scope
IATF 16949 defines the quality management system requirements for organizations in the automotive supply chain. It is based on ISO 9001:2015 with additional automotive-specific requirements.

## Key Requirements

### Product Design and Development
- Design and development planning must include APQP (Advanced Product Quality Planning)
- Design FMEA per AIAG-VDA FMEA handbook
- Design validation per customer-specific requirements

### Process Design and Development
- Process Flow Diagrams
- PFMEA (Process Failure Mode and Effects Analysis)
- Control Plan (per AIAG Control Plan manual)
- Work Instructions
- Process approval per PPAP (Production Part Approval Process)

### Production
- Statistical Process Control (SPC) per AIAG SPC manual
- Measurement Systems Analysis (MSA) per AIAG MSA manual
- First-article inspection
- Lot traceability
- Error-proofing (Poka-Yoke)

### Key Metrics
- PPM (defects per million parts)
- OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)
- Scrap rate
- Rework rate
- Customer complaints (PPM)
- On-time delivery

## Documentation Requirements
- Quality Manual
- 21 mandatory procedures and records
- Control Plan for each part/process
- PFMEA with risk prioritization (RPN or AP)
- PPAP submission at Level 2, 3, or 4

## Audits
- Internal QMS audit annually
- Internal process audit per scheduled rotation
- Product audit per defined frequency
- Second-party (supplier) audits
- Third-party (certification body) audits every 3 years
